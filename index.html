ABOUT US

The collaboration between Davide Masci and Sofia Poccioni began spontaneously and genuinely in September 2022, despite differences in age, background, tastes, and personal interests.

This chemistry is reflected in their works, which represent the outcome of a shared creative process in which each of the two contributes their skills and personality.

The ViRVAR duo invites you to immerse yourself in a world where emotions blend and boundaries dissolve.

POETICS

ViRVAR's poetics ("chaos" in Danish) blend the fantastical world with the grotesque, creating an engaging visual experience.

ViRVAR's works are notable for their fusion of various artistic languages, particularly collage, which allows the merging of diverse materials and images.

The dimensions range from small pieces measuring 10 × 10 cm to larger compositions of 50 × 40 cm, providing a variety of perspectives and details that enhance their visual impact.
